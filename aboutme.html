About Me Page

My name is Kade Skogman. I am a student at High Point University located in High Point, North Carolina, and I'm pursuing a degree in Social Media and Digital Communications. I am originally from a small town in Iowa called Marion, which has a population of about 45,000 people. I lived in Marion until I graduated from Washington High School in 2022. My junior year of high school, I was still unsure if I wanted to attend college, and my parents finally convinced me to attend a two-year community college, which had become my plan. I was soon convinced to visit High Point, and I immediately fell in love. Little did I know I would end up moving to High Point for the school year and going back home during the summers. I enjoy hiking and spending time with friends and family. After graduating from HPU, I plan to work for my family real estate company back home and get into land development.
